The skeleton joint order in UTD-MAD dataset:

1. head; 
2. shoulder_center;
3. spine;
4. hip_center;
5. left_shoulder;
6. left_elbow;
7. left_wrist;
8. left_hand;
9. right_shoulder;
10. right_elbow;
11. right_wrist;
12. right_hand;
13. left_hip;
14. left_knee;
15. left_ankle;
16. left_foot;
17. right_hip;
18. right_knee;
19. right_ankle;
20. right_foot;

Each skeleton data is a 20 x 3 x num_frame matrix. Each row of a skeleton frame corresponds to three spatial coordinates of a joint.
